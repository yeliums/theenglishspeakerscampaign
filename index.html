TESC About

Introduction

The English Speakers Campaign is a workshop and activity based learning campaign organized by Yeliums, The Youth Originals. The campaign aims to improve the english oriented skills of the youth through organized tasks. It is a self-paced campaign where, the participants will do the assigned tasks with our provided supplements on their own to master certain topics which is a part of our NUET Theory. It is a virtual campaign of 25 days duration with a time limit of 1 hour each day.

In Brief

Yeliums, The Youth Originals is going to organize its first campaign called “The English Speakers Campaign” of 25 days’ duration from January 2 to 26, 2023. It will be organized and conducted virtually easing the participant’s travel problem. Also, the tasks are well organized and more simplified taking care of the time and the study hindrance issue.

‘The English Speakers Campaign’ by Yeliums has been organized to improve the English with other relevant skills. The campaign includes; activities, notes, tips and new topics with grammars, pronunciations, list of vocabularies & idioms on the skills Reading, Writing and Speaking. Debating is also included to aid the Speaking Activity. Also new topics and notes outside the academic lecture will be introduced.

The activities with other relevant things mentioned above are the core of the Campaign, I.e. participants will do the activities with the provided academics and guidance to excel their English skills. (That’s why, we termed this Campaign a practical approach.)

Participant's Gain

For participants, there are a bunch of perks in the campaign. You will get the mentioned academics and will be introduced to new topics of English. In addition, the only reason of the duration to be such long is that, you have to invest only 1hr each day to the 25 days Campaign.

Furthermore, there some awards and acknowledgement for the best participants (we care for your dedication and talent). For instance; Best Speakers/ Writers/ Debaters and a lot that goes with it.
